# Admin Phase 36: Operations workflow streamlining

Phase 36 turns ten dense Operations destinations into one calm, outcome-led entry point while preserving every detailed workboard and backend action.

## Operations Center

- Replaces ten expanded sidebar links with one Operations Center entry.
- Organizes the work into four human lanes: Plan the Work, Protect Client Care, Complete the Session Loop, and Review What Changed.
- Offers a short recommended path from orientation to action to confirmation.
- Applies the existing staff permission model before showing a tool.
- Keeps every detailed route available through the Operations Center, Quick Find, pins, direct links, and the current-workspace state.

## Compact workboards

- Keeps the primary search field visible on the eight densest operational boards.
- Starts secondary filters collapsed behind one More Filters control.
- Shows an active-filter count when a hidden filter is affecting the result.
- Preserves familiar filter choices and does not reset them when the panel closes.
- Adapts the disclosure and lane layouts for desktop, tablet, and mobile widths.

## Preserved efficiency

- Week, Capacity, and Attention retain work ownership, scheduling, status, and completion updates.
- Momentum, Coverage, Readiness, and Follow-Through retain their complete backend datasets and detail links.
- Studio Activity retains read, mark-all-read, dismiss, and clear-read controls.
- Activity Journal remains protected by its existing audit permission.
